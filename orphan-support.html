Orphan and Widow Support

Caring for the Most Vulnerable

Supporting Vulnerable Families

AOHF provides comprehensive support to orphans and widows, offering education, healthcare, livelihood assistance, and emotional support. We work to ensure these vulnerable families have the resources and opportunities they need to build stable, hopeful futures.

Our Support Services

  • Educational Support School fees, supplies, and tutoring for orphaned children
  • Healthcare Access Medical care and health insurance for families
  • Livelihood Programs Skills training and small business support for widows
  • Psychosocial Support Counseling and emotional care for grieving families
