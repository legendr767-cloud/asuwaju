Mosque Construction

Building Houses of Worship

Building Spiritual Centers

AOHF supports the construction and renovation of mosques in underserved communities, providing dignified places of worship where communities can gather for prayer, learning, and spiritual growth. These projects strengthen community bonds and provide essential religious infrastructure.

Our Construction Services

  • New Mosque Construction Building mosques in communities without worship facilities
  • Mosque Renovation Restoring and upgrading existing prayer facilities
  • Ablution Facilities Installing proper wudu areas with clean water
  • Community Spaces Creating areas for Islamic education and gatherings
